About the ICI

The Immigrant Climate Index measures the regulation-induced "climate" for immigrants in the United States at federal, state, county, and city levels through a quantitative scoring system.

Positive laws (sanctuary laws) are immigrant-friendly. Restrictive laws limit immigrant rights or increase enforcement.
